```
# A new instance of `<application>` is installed

## Supported Languages:
- de-AT (German, Austria)
- de-CH (German, Switzerland)
- de-DE (German, Germany)
- de (German)
- en (English)
- es-419 (Spanish, Latin America and the Caribbean)
- fr-FR (French, France)
- it-IT (Italian, Italy)
- ja (Japanese)
- pl-PL (Polish, Poland)
- pt-BR (Portuguese, Brazil)
- tr-TR (Turkish, Turkey)
```

Step 1:
```
### Verify the Consent Management Prompt (CMP) is localized:
1. Open the OS settings app
2. Navigate to "General > Language & Region"
3. Update the device language to `<supported language>`
4. Launch app from a fresh install
```
